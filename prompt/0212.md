## 修改yfjcgkzx\hqzcsj\templates\zfba_jq_aj_tab.html中的"警情","同比警情"数据源sql
    - 基础查询如下,其中"calltime"字段过滤时间,"ctc.leixing"过滤类型:
        ```sql
            SELECT
                jq."calltime" AS "报警时间",
                jq."caseno" AS "警情编号",
                jq."dutydeptname" AS "管辖单位",
                jq."cmdname" AS "分局",
                jq."occuraddress" AS "警情地址",
                jq."casecontents" AS "报警内容",
                jq."replies" AS "处警情况",
                jq."casemark" AS "警情标注",
                jq."lngofcriterion" AS "经度",
                jq."latofcriterion" AS "纬度",
                LEFT(jq."cmdid", 6) AS "地区",
                zza."ajxx_ajbh" "案件编号",
                zza."ajxx_lasj" 立案时间,
                zza."ajxx_cbdw_mc" 办案单位
            FROM ywdata."zq_kshddpt_dsjfx_jq" jq
            LEFT JOIN "zq_zfba_ajxx" zza ON jq."caseno" =zza."ajxx_jqbh" 
            WHERE 1=1 
            AND jq."calltime" BETWEEN '2025-01-01' AND '2025-02-02'
            AND jq.neworicharasubclass IN (
                SELECT unnest(ctc.newcharasubclass_list) 
                FROM ywdata.case_type_config ctc 
                WHERE ctc.leixing = ANY('打架斗殴'))
        ```
